# Pharmaceutical Lab Products and Efficiency

One form of industry in Starfield is producing drugs. Some drugs are able to be produced at industrial scale, with input products scalable by number of extractors/greenhouses/animal houses, while other drugs will rely on availability of source materials such as Trauma Packs, Chasmbass Oil, or Caelumite (loot, vendor, anomaly sites respectively).

The industrial base for this plan is described in *Industry From Scratch*.

## The Lab

First up, let's look at what we can produce. For making money, we'll look at the highest Credits per kilogram. For supporting gameplay we'll add some interesting chems like Snake Oil and Repairing Immobilizer which will cover the bases in terms of curing afflictions.

## Planet Plans

### Serpentis IV

This is the main lab, with production facilities and large solid storages for each component.

In general you need about 1 water extractor for each greenhouse and Animal Husbandry Facility (AHF). Each AHF in this installation requires fibre too. This means that for each animal husbandry facility there are 2 water extractors and 1 greenhouse producing fiber. Then you find that production rates never quite match up with consumption rates, but fine tuning is something that can happen later.

NB: The greenhouses and animal facilities can be replaced with industrial variants as part of *Advanced Reactor Assembly Line* - skip to the section on *Industrial Liquid Extractors*. Short version: buy Substrate Molecule Sieve from Clint/Outland/UC Exchange/Sieghart/Shepard's/The Trader, and replace 2 Greenhouse with 1 Greenhouse - Industrial. For the water extractors, you also want Veryl-Treated Manifold from the same vendors.

Design:

- 5 Wind Turbine - Advanced (25 power)
- 8 Animal Husbandry Facility (analgesic, 4 membrane, sealant, spice, toxin)
- 12 Greenhouse (9 fiber, metabolic agent, nutrient, structural)
- 15 Extractor - Liquid (water)
- Storage - Liquid - Large (water)
- 13 Storage - Solid - Large (analgesic, antimicrobial, aromatic, biosuppressant, fiber, 2 membrane, metabolic agent, nutrient, sealant, sedative, spice, toxin)
- 3 Extractor - Gas (argon, benzene, chlorine)
- 4 Storage - Gas - Large (alkanes, argon, benzene, chlorine)
- Pharmaceutical Lab (analgesic poultice)

Materials:

- Manufactured
  - Adaptive Frame: 270

### Alpha Andraste III (biosuppressant, hallucinogen, Xe)

This base exists to extract two resources: Biosuppressant and Xenon. For the pharmaceuticals lab the only thing we care about is biosuppressant (repairing immobiliser) unless you're also planning to produce Aurora in which case collect hallucinogen too. Xenon is useful for Laser Turret Mk III, and outpost lighting. You can probably collect enough Xenon to last the entire game just manually collecting it from vents as you fully survey Alpha Andraste III.

- 1 Wind Turbine - Advanced (25 power)
- 1 Landing Pad with Shipbuilder

## Operation

From time to time, do a tour of the supply planets to pick up ingredients:

- Procyon III (antimicrobial, sedative)
- Alpha Andraste III (biosuppressant)
- Groombridge VIII-a/Hyla II (aromatic)
- Alpha Tirna VIII-c (alkanes)

Then cook up:

- My preferred food
  - Alien Liquor (O2/CO2 recovery)
  - Alien Stew (damage resistance)
- Amp
- Analgesic Poultice
- Antibiotic Injector
- Repairing Immobiliser
- Snake Oil

You can easily automate this collection with cargo links. I prefer doing the rounds manually since it's all relatively low volume and I like the change in scenery from Serpentis IV.
